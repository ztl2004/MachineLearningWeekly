The brain can process images seen for just 13 milliseconds¶

A smart-object recognition algorithm that doesn’t need humans¶

BYU教授搞的nb识别算法，号称无监督，自动做feature selection，称为Evolution-COnstructed (ECO) features。

不过话说都不考虑recall的么？
